Hello There!

Welcome to the official website of the Youth Red Cross (YRC) at Rajalakshmi Engineering College! We are a dynamic and compassionate community committed to fostering humanitarian values and making a positive impact on society. Explore our site to discover our initiatives, events, and resources aimed at promoting social welfare, health awareness, and community service. Join us in our mission to empower and inspire the youth to create a better world!.

Awareness Programs

Youth Red Cross REC conducts awareness programs and campaigns on various social issues, health-related topics, and humanitarian causes.

Health and First Aid Training

Youth Red Cross of REC organizes workshops and training sessions on first aid, basic life support, and disaster management.

Blood Donation Drives

Youth Red Cross Rajalakshmi Engineering College actively promotes blood donation and often organizes blood donation camps on campus.

Community Service

YRC REC participates in visiting orphanages, old age homes encourage students and staff to voluntarily donate blood.

Social Outreach Programs

YRC REC collaborates with local NGOs and organizations to conduct social outreach programs like awareness campaigns.

YRC REC Awarded Best Youth Red Cross Team in Kancheepuram District

We are thrilled to announce that our dedicated Youth Red Cross (YRC) team at Rajalakshmi Engineering College has been honored with the prestigious title of the district's finest. This recognition is a testament to our unwavering commitment to community service, impactful initiatives, and unwavering dedication to social welfare. From organizing awareness campaigns to blood donation drives and collaborating with local NGOs, we continuously strive to make a positive impact. This achievement is a reflection of our passion, hard work, and relentless spirit in serving humanity. Heartfelt congratulations to our extraordinary YRC REC team! Together, we will continue to make a difference. #YRCREC #BestYRCTeam #CommunityService #MakingADifference
